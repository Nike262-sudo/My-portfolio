01 — Overview

The exploration

A design exploration in minimalism — clean layouts, strong typography, and intentional spacing.

02 — Goal

Design intent

  • —Explore minimalist design principles in a real-world context
  • —Improve readability through intentional typographic choices
  • —Create a balanced and visually calm interface
  • —Focus on clarity over complexity at every decision point
03 — Design Decisions

What shaped the work

Every decision was made in service of one idea: less, but better.

Minimal Layout
Layout stripped to essentials — every section has one clear purpose.
Typography
Type as a design element. Hierarchy, scale, and spacing guide the user without decoration.
Spacing & Balance
Generous white space creates visual rhythm and draws the eye to what matters.
Visual Restraint
Black and white palette only — restraint forces stronger structural decisions.
